Operating

Facility Overview

Ascend Elements' Base 1 facility in Covington, Georgia, represents a significant investment in sustainable battery recycling and materials production. This advanced manufacturing facility specializes in transforming end-of-life batteries and manufacturing scrap into high-quality battery materials using the company's proprietary Hydro-to-Cathode™ technology.

The Georgia facility processes lithium-ion batteries from electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and energy storage systems, recovering critical materials such as lithium, nickel, and cobalt. What distinguishes Ascend Elements is its vertically integrated approach, which enables the transformation of recycled battery materials directly into new cathode active materials for lithium-ion batteries.

Base 1 serves as a cornerstone of Ascend Elements' expansion in the southeastern United States, a region that has attracted significant investment in electric vehicle and battery manufacturing. The facility supports the growing demand for sustainably produced battery materials and contributes to creating a closed-loop battery supply chain in North America.

Technical Specifications

  • Processing Capacity: 30,000 tonnes per year
  • Technology: Hydro-to-Cathode™ direct recycling
  • Operational Since: 2022
  • Feedstock: End-of-life batteries and manufacturing scrap
  • Output: Cathode active materials and battery precursors
  • Facility Size: 154,000 square feet

Environmental Impact

  • Water Management: Integrated water recycling systems
  • Waste Diversion: Near-zero waste to landfill operations
  • Resource Recovery: >95% recovery rate for critical materials
  • Energy Efficiency: Lower energy requirements than traditional cathode manufacturing

Company Information

Ascend Elements is a leading provider of sustainable battery material solutions that transform the lithium-ion battery supply chain. The company's Hydro-to-Cathode™ technology directly synthesizes new cathode active material from spent lithium-ion cells, enabling more efficient recovery of critical battery metals and reducing the environmental impact of battery production.

Processing Technology

The Base 1 facility utilizes Ascend Elements' Hydro-to-Cathode™ direct recycling process:

  • Initial battery preparation and safe discharge
  • Shredding and mechanical separation of components
  • Proprietary hydrometallurgical processing
  • Direct synthesis of cathode precursor materials
  • Advanced calcination processes
  • Quality control and material validation

Regional Significance

The Base 1 facility is strategically located in Georgia's growing battery and electric vehicle manufacturing corridor. The southeastern United States has attracted major investments from automotive manufacturers and battery producers, creating a robust ecosystem for battery materials. The facility contributes to local economic development by creating skilled jobs and supports the establishment of a domestic battery supply chain, reducing dependency on imported materials.
