Cirba Solutions Arizona Facility

Operating

Facility Overview

The Cirba Solutions Arizona Facility is located in Eloy, Arizona, and serves as a strategic processing center for battery recycling in the southwestern United States. With approximately 75,000 square feet of operating space, this facility is designed to process and recycle various types of batteries, with a growing focus on lithium-ion batteries from electric vehicles and energy storage systems.

The Arizona facility is well-positioned to serve the growing battery manufacturing ecosystem in the Southwest region, including nearby operations in Nevada and California. It employs a range of processing technologies to safely handle, sort, and process batteries to recover valuable materials for reuse in the battery supply chain.

As part of Cirba Solutions' national network of recycling facilities, the Eloy operation plays an important role in creating a resilient, domestic battery materials supply chain. The facility's operations align with the company's commitment to sustainable practices and circular economy principles in battery recycling.

  • Operational Since: Pre-2021

Processing Technology

The Arizona facility uses a comprehensive approach to battery recycling:

  • Safe battery discharge and handling
  • Automated sorting of various battery types
  • Mechanical processing systems for size reduction
  • Material separation technologies
  • Black mass production and preparation
  • Quality control and material categorization

Regional Significance

The Arizona facility is strategically located to serve the southwestern United States, including growing EV and battery manufacturing operations in Nevada, Arizona, and California. The Eloy location benefits from proximity to transportation routes and supports the development of a regional battery recycling ecosystem. The facility contributes to local employment and economic development while reducing the environmental impact of battery disposal in the region.
